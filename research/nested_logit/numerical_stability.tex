Nested Logit
\[

  \frac{e^{V_{ni}/\lambda_{k}}
  \left(\sum_{j\in B_{k}}e^{V_{nj}/\lambda_{k}}\right)
  ^{\lambda_{k}-1}}
  {\sum_{l=1}^{K}\left(\sum_{j\in B_{l}}e^{V_{nj}/\lambda_{l}}\right)^{\lambda_{l}}}
\]
